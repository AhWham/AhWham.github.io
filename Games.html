Project Fling Cube

A short little platformer I made as my first project in Godot, There are only 15 levels of increasing difficultly and is meant to be completed in one sitting. The whole thing is designed around you testing out this cube you can fling around, that is it I didn't want to go too in depth with any premise since I wanted this game to be simple.
